About FastPay

Headquartered in Los Angeles, CA, FastPay is a tech-enabled finance acceleration platform that helps digital businesses overcome the struggle of long receivable cycles by providing specialized credit lines.

By combining proprietary technology with industry experience, we can quickly assess the creditworthiness of a digital business and provide loans ranging from $100k to several million dollars in under 48 hours to help their customers effectively manage their cash flow. FastPay has already delivered over $130 million to businesses across the entire digital ecosystem.

Our Team

Jed Simon (Founder/CEO)

Jed began his career in the Investment Banking New Media Group at Morgan Stanley & Co. in New York providing IPO, debt finance, and merger and acquisitions advisory services prior to joining DreamWorks SKG in 1996. During his ten year career at DreamWorks, Jed managed aspects of its finance, distribution, marketing, and new media businesses, and was a senior executive at DreamWorks Records before assisting in the sale of the company to Universal Music Group. His last position with DreamWorks was Vice President of International Distribution and Marketing for DreamWorks Pictures, based in London. Jed graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Brown University and is on the Board of Directors for Brown University Sports Foundation and is active with the Los Angeles County Museum of Contemporary Art and Big Brothers & Big Sisters of Los Angeles.

Mennen Coleman (Chief Credit Officer)

Mennen began her career in the CIT training program where she spent rotations in the Client Credit, New Business Underwriting, Customer Credit, Audit, and Collateral Monitoring departments. Mennen later was promoted to Account Executive and Client Service Officer, managing a portfolio with approximately $500mil in annual volume. Mennen joined Celtic Capital, a Los Angeles-based Asset-Based Lending institution, working as the Vice President of Credit managing high risk clients. At FastPay, Mennen has been implementing improved policies and procedures for more systematic risk analysis and operational controls. Mennen earned a BA in Business from California State University, Long Beach.

Tim Gooch (Controller)

Tim leads the finance and accounting operations at FastPay, working closely with our operations, underwriting, business development and technology teams. Prior to joining FastPay, Tim worked at Buzzmedia, a leading web publisher, as Director of Finance/Controller where he was responsible for the financial and accounting operations of the company. Prior to that, he worked as Director of Finance at Movielink. Tim began his career at PricewaterhouseCoopers, where he provided financial advisory and accounting assurance services to companies in the technology and entertainment sectors. Tim holds a bachelor's in accounting and finance from California Polytechnic University, San Luis Obispo.

Mark Baran (Senior Director of Product)

Mark is responsible for product management of new technologies and lending products. As product manager, Mark plans systems to automate the sales funnel, facilitate the sales process, and generally manage portfolio risk, both programmatically and from a process perspective. He also leads FastPay's metrics and analytic efforts. Mark is a 2010 graduate of Harvard University, Cum Laude, where he majored in an interdisciplinary curriculum combining Economics and Philosophy.

TJ Hennessy (Director of Business Development)

Jake Skinner (Director of Strategic Planning)

Jake is responsible for managing our product development strategy in tandem with our technology, credit, and business development teams.
